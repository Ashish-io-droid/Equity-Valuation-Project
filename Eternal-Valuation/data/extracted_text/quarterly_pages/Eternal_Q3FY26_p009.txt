014. What is leading to the (almost consistent) ~INR 50 crore quarterly loss in the 
‘Others’ segment? 
Akshant: The quarterly losses in the Others segment are largely on account of investments in our quick 
food delivery service Bistro, where kitchen infrastructure is managed by us. We now have about 45 
kitchens operational mainly in two cities (Delhi NCR, Bangalore). We are seeing early signs of 
product-market-fit, reflected in healthy throughput per outlet and early signs of a possible path to 
profitability.
