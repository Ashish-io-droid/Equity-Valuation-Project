Key takeaways 
1. Quick commerce and Hyperpure businesses turn Adjusted EBITDA profitable in Q3FY26. 
2. Quick commerce NOV growth remains robust at 121% YoY; growth negatively impacted by 
GST changes and seasonality with like for like NOV growth at 130%+ YoY. 
3. Food delivery NOV growth accelerates to 16.6% YoY sustaining the growth momentum after 
bottoming out at 13.1% YoY in Q1FY26; Adjusted EBITDA margin at all-time high of 5.4%. 
4. District losses increase on account of continued investments in category creation; expect 
losses to reduce from here sequentially towards breakeven in the next 4-6 quarters.
