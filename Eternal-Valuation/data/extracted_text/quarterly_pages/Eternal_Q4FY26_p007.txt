reduced to INR 81 crore vs INR 121 crore loss in Q3FY26. As a result, Adjusted EBITDA margin (as a % of NOV) 
improved to -3.0% from -4.7% in Q3FY26. 
Hyperpure's restaurant supply Revenue growth improves to 37% YoY vs 33% in the previous quarter, with

crore (as compared to INR 1crore in the previous quarter). 
Ata consolidated level, Adjusted Revenue grew 186% YoY (6% QoQ)to INR 17,680 crore, though like-for-like
