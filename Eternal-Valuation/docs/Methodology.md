# Methodology

## Valuation Methods

The final valuation will use:

- Discounted Cash Flow
- Trading Comparable Companies
- Precedent Transactions, only if transaction data is credible
- Football field synthesis

## DCF Framework

DCF will estimate enterprise value using:

- Forecast revenue
- EBITDA margin
- D&A
- Capex
- Change in net working capital
- Cash taxes
- Unlevered free cash flow
- WACC
- Terminal value via Gordon Growth and Exit Multiple

## Relative Valuation

Trading comps will focus on food delivery, quick commerce, local commerce, and consumer internet peers where data is comparable.

Precedents will be used as a cross-check only when transaction value and revenue / EBITDA bases are auditable.

## Investment Decision

The final recommendation will include:

- Why buy
- Why hold
- Why sell
- Catalysts
- Risks
- What would change the valuation
